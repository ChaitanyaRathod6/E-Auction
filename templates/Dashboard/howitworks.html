Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about bidding, selling, and using Auctora.

How do I register on Auctora? ▾
Click "Get Started" in the top navigation, enter your email, and follow the KYC verification steps. Approval is typically instant for buyers and under 24 hours for sellers.
Is my payment information secure? ▾
Yes. All payments are processed through Stripe with AES-256 encryption. We never store your card details on our servers. Every transaction is protected by our escrow system.
What happens if I win an auction? ▾
You'll receive an email notification immediately. You have 48 hours to complete payment. Once paid, the seller ships your item with full insurance and tracking. We authenticate every item before it leaves the seller.
Can I cancel a bid after placing it? ▾
Bids on Auctora are binding once placed. In exceptional circumstances (such as a listing error), you can contact our support team within 30 minutes of placing a bid for review.
How does auto-bidding work? ▾
When you enable auto-bid, you set a maximum amount you're willing to pay. Our system automatically increments your bid by the minimum increment whenever you're outbid — up to your maximum — so you never miss a win.
What fees does Auctora charge sellers? ▾
Sellers pay a 5% commission on the final sale price. There are no listing fees. Premium promoted listing slots are available for an additional fee. Full fee schedule is available in your seller dashboard.
What countries does Auctora support? ▾
Auctora is available in 60+ countries across North America, Europe, Asia-Pacific, and the Middle East. Shipping rates and availability vary by item and seller location.
