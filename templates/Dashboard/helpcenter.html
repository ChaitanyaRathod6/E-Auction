Frequently Asked Questions

Q. How are items authenticated?

All items are physically inspected at our secure regional hubs by a panel of verified experts before the auction goes live.

Q. What are the buyer's fees?

Our standard buyer's premium is 12% for items under $100k, and 8% for items above $100k.

Q. Can I cancel a bid?

Bids are legally binding commitments. Cancellations are only permitted in extreme cases within 15 minutes of the bid.

Q. How long does shipping take?

Domestic shipping takes 3-5 business days. International shipping typically ranges from 7-14 days depending on customs.
