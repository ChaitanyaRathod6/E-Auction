Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click "Create account" in the top right corner, fill in your details, and verify your email address. It's free and takes less than 2 minutes.

How does bidding work?

Enter your maximum bid, and our system will automatically bid incrementally on your behalf up to that amount. You'll be notified if you're outbid.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, and bank transfers for verified accounts.

How do I sell an item?

Create a seller account, verify your identity, then click "List Item" to start your auction. Add photos, description, and set your starting price.

What are the seller fees?

Listing fees start at $0.50, and final value fees range from 5-10% depending on the category. View our full fee schedule in your seller dashboard.

How do I track my shipment?

Once shipped, tracking information will appear in your order history. You'll also receive email updates with tracking links.

Can I cancel a bid?

Bids are binding, but you can retract within the first hour if you made a mistake. After that, contact the seller directly.

What is buyer protection?

If your item doesn't arrive or isn't as described, we'll help resolve the issue and can refund your purchase up to $2,500.

Still need help?

Our support team is available 24/7 to answer your questions
