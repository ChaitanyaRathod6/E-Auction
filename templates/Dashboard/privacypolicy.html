1. Introduction

Welcome to Auctora. Your privacy is paramount to our operation. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, process, and disclose your information, including personal information, in conjunction with your access to and use of the Auctora Platform.

When this policy mentions "Auctora," "we," "us," or "our," it refers to the Auctora company that is responsible for your information under this Privacy Policy.
